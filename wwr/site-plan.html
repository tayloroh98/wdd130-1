Overview

Purpose

How long does it take you to plan your trip?
I have seen many people who spend too much time and effort planning their trip.
Their problem is they don't know where get the trip information. The website is aimed at helping the struggling to plan more easily.
Inside this website, it can be seen as special in that when they click on the information they need,
they can see all the websites that they can refer for a trip into a display that is easy to see at a glance.

Audience

people who are struggling with planning

Content

Home page

People basically have a desire for food, clothing, and shelter, but I think people also have a strong desire for travel.
It can be given as a reward to oneself who usually works hard, but it can be seen that many people spend too much time planning
the trip. These are activities they do to make themselves happy, and at some point, they can find themselves stressed out.
Therefore, the website will help you enjoy happier trips by reducing the effort and stress needed to go to a specific area
and make travel plans. To do so, we will gather necessary information such as culture, food, language, and scenery of a specific region
in one place so that people can use it conveniently.

Images for the Home page

[Page 2]

The question I think of my visitors is usually 'What should I do when I go?', I think there are things like
"What about the people there?" and "What attractions do they have?" And my website will organize the information
needed to answer all of these questions in one place and help me solve them right here without having to go to another website
and find another.

Images for the Page 2

[Page 3]

The reason why my customers visit my website without visiting other websites is that our website has information
that other websites do not provide. If you look at most travel companies' websites, their information is place-oriented
and provides information focusing on entertainment because they are companies made for commercial purposes.
However, since my website is originally for the public interest, not for the personal interest, it can be seen that it is focused on
providing the information we need to travel rather than the information we want. At first, of course, it may be difficult for people to attract attention
because they cannot provide the information they want right away, but when they look at the website with an active attitude,
hey can see the authenticity of this website and I think this can gradually bring customers.
